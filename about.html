ORTHOPEDIC & SPORTS INJURY CLINIC

Orthopedic & Sports Injury Clinic was started under the visionary leadership of Dr. V R Sujit Kumar in the city of Hyderabad with the goal to be a centre of excellence for all Orthopedic and sports related injuries. Dr. Sujit completed his MBBS from Mamata Medical College, Khammam. Following which he pursued and attained M.S. (Ortho) from Deccan College of Medical Sciences, Hyderabad. To achieve further degree of excellence, Dr. Sujit completed his M.Ch. (Ortho) from American Institute of Medical, University of Sychelles.

Hunger to learn and excel in the field of orthopedics led Dr. Sujit to attain his Fellowship in Advanced Arthroscopy in Knee and shoulder at Yashoda Hospitals, Secundrabad. Also, he has achieved Shoulder and Elbow fellowship in Funabashi Orthopedic Hospital Japan in 2017. He worked as a senior resident in Osmania General Hospital, Hyderabad. Later, as an assistant professor in Deccan College of Medical Sciences, Hyderabad and in GSL Medical College, Rajahmundry. Dr. Sujit is currently extending his knowledge to the next generations by working as an Associate professor in Mamata Academy of Medical Sciences Hospital.
